Strategic Recommendations
Data-driven actions for Q2 2026 based on Q1 performance
🎓 High Priority
Double Down on EduTrust Content
The EduTrust certification reel drove 7,466 views and 86,840 seconds viewed — the highest of any Q1 post and well above average. Create follow-up EduTrust content: parent FAQs, what EduTrust means for students, and testimonial videos.
📊 #1 post: 7,466 views · 86,840 seconds viewed
📸 High Priority
Activate & Grow Instagram
Instagram reach (14,586) is only 9% of Facebook's (160,565), yet both audiences share near-identical demographics. Systematically repurpose top Facebook posts as IG Reels. Set a weekly posting cadence with Stories for event coverage.
📊 IG reach = 9% of FB · Same audience demo
💰 High Priority
Measure Event ROI vs Lead Attribution
51.7% of Q1 spend ($3,737) went to events and sponsorships — the single largest cost center. However only 12 event walk-in leads were captured. Implement strict lead capture at all events in Q2 to measure true cost-per-lead.
📊 $3,737 events · only 12 walk-in leads captured · 0.84% conversion rate
💬 Medium Priority
Strengthen WhatsApp & Form CTAs
Contact Us Form (43 leads, 36.1%) and WhatsApp (34 leads, 28.6%) are the top two lead channels. Add a WhatsApp click-to-chat button in every Facebook post caption and email footer. Ensure all form confirmations have an immediate follow-up auto-reply.
📊 Contact Form + WhatsApp = 64.7% of all Q1 leads
📈 Medium Priority
Sustain the March Momentum
March was the strongest month across all metrics: FB reach +32% vs Feb, FB interactions +411% vs Feb, LinkedIn impressions 6× vs January. Identify and document what drove this surge (EduTrust news, event mix, posting frequency) and replicate the playbook in Q2.
📊 Mar FB interactions: 2,105 vs Jan: 784 (+168%)
✉️ Medium Priority
Scale High-Performing Email Formats
EduTrust EOI had the highest CTR at 13.92% — nearly 10× the average CTR. The template combined urgency (limited spots), social proof (certification), and a clear CTA. Apply this formula to upcoming Open House and enrollment deadline campaigns.
📊 EduTrust EOI: 13.92% CTR vs avg ~5.3%
💼 Growth Insight
Invest More in LinkedIn
LinkedIn impressions grew 502% from January (226) to March (1,359) with near-zero incremental spend. This is a high-value channel for reaching Singapore-based parents and education professionals. Increase posting frequency to 3× per week and consider a small sponsored content budget in Q2.
📊 LinkedIn impressions: +502% Jan→Mar · Low cost
